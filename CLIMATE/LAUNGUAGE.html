PEOPLE AND POPULATION.

A.Launguage groups in Kenya.

A launguage is a group is a group of people who speak the same or similer languages.

Launguage groups include:

  1. Bantus
  2. Nilotes
  3. Cushites
  4. Semites
  5. Europeans
  6. Asians

BANTUS

Bantus are the largest language group in Kenya.Migrated from the Congo Forest. They were Farmers/Cultivaters.

Bantus are devided in to two groups.

  1. Western Bantus
  2. Eastern Bantus

Western Bantus

  1. Abalugha
  2. Abagusii
  3. Abakuria
  4. Abasuba

Eastern Bantus are divided into two.

  1. Central Bantus
  2. Costal Bantus

Central Bantus

  1. Ameru
  2. Akamba
  3. Ambeere
  4. Aembu
  5. Agikuyu

Costal Bantus

  1. Mjikenda
  2. Waswahili
  3. Wadawida
  4. Wapokomo
  5. Wataita

NILOTES

They migrated from Bah-el ghazel or South Sudan.Their economic activity is livestock keeping.

They are divided into three groups:

  1. River lake nilotes.
  2. Plain nilotes.
  3. High land nilotes

Plain Nilotes

  1. Maasai
  2. Turkana
  3. Samburu
  4. Njimps
  5. Iteso
  6. Karamanjong

These are the plain nilotes.

River Lakes Nilotes.

  1. Luo

Highland Nilotes(Kalenjins).

  1. PO-Poket
  2. KI-Kipsigis
  3. SA-Sabaot
  4. NA-Nandi
  5. MA-Marakwet
  6. Ke-Keiyo
  7. TU-Tugen

CUSHITES

They migrated from Somaliland or Horn of Africa.They are livestock keepers.Divided into two.

a.Eastern cushites

  1. Borana
  2. Burji
  3. Somalia
  4. Oromo
  5. Gabbra
  6. Rendile

b.Southern Cushites

  1. Sanye
  2. Dahalo
