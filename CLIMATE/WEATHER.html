WEATHER AND CLIMATE IN KENYA.

Weather is a state of an atmosphere at a particular place.It is also the hotness or coldness of a place.Climate is an average conditions of weather in an area.Climate cannot change but weather changes from day to day.Climate is described as wet,dry,hot,cold,warm or cool.

Elements of weather.

    a) RAINY
    b) SUNNY
    c) WINDY
    d) CLOUDY

Climatic regions in Kenya.

Kenya is divided into 5 climatic regions.

1. Hot and wet climatic region.

It is found in Coastal and Lake Victoria.

Temperatures are very high.

It receives convectional rainfall.

The region is hot and wet throughout the year.

Hot and wet climatic region is also known as equatorial climatic region.

This kind of climate is experienced in areas such as Mombasa and Kisumu.

2.Cool and wet climatic region.

It is also called modified tropical climate.

It receives relief rainfall because of the altitude.

It receives alot of rainfall.

The high altitude causes the temperatures to be lower.

The area is cold and wet through out the year.

This climate is experienced in areas such as Kisii,Nairobi,Kitale and Kericho.

3.Tropical climate region.

The region has high temperatures.

It has one rainy season.

It covers regions of Kwale,Narok and Taita.

4. Desert and Semidesert climate.

It is also called Arid and Semiarid climatic regions.

It covers Northern and Eastern parts of Kenya e.g Turkana,Isiolo,Samburu etc.

The areas are Hot and Dry throughout the year.

It receives low rainfall.

High temperatures during the day and low during the night.

MOUNTAIN CLIMATIC REGION

5. Mountain climatic region.

It covers the highest points of the mountains.

The areas are cold and wet throughout the year.

It receives a relief rainfall.

It has two sides the leeward and the windward side.

The rain shadow is known as the leeward side and the windward side receives rainfall.



THESE ARE KENYA'S CLIMATIC REGIONS.
